# Mode: training — Training Evaluation

For each course/cert the candidate asks about, evaluate 6 dimensions:

| Dimension | What it evaluates |
|-----------|-------------------|
| North Star alignment | Does it bring closer or further from the goal? |
| Recruiter signal | What do HMs think when they see this on a CV? |
| Time and effort | Weeks × hours/week |
| Opportunity cost | What can't the candidate do during that time? |
| Risks | Outdated content? Weak brand? Too basic? |
| Portfolio deliverable | Does it produce a demonstrable artifact? |

## Verdicts

- **DO IT** → 4-12 week plan with weekly deliverables and scoreboard
- **DON'T DO IT** → better alternative with justification
- **DO IT WITH TIMEBOX** (max X weeks) → condensed plan, only the essentials

## Priority

Training that improves credibility in backend engineering at scale:
1. System design and distributed systems
2. Cloud architecture certifications (AWS Solutions Architect, etc.)
3. Performance optimization and observability
4. Security and compliance
5. Database internals and storage systems
